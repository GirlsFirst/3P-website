What is FRC

FRC is a competive robotics competionnwhere students build a robot during build season and then use the robot to complete challenges of the years competion

What positions are avaliable in a FRC teams?

Coding: Coders are in charge of programing the robot with movement, sensors, and buttons so that the robot can funnction

Build: Build team is in charge of building the robot. They take care of wiring, building the habitat model and game piece model for pratice, and builidng the hardware

Scouting/Stratgey: Scouters/Stratigies will talk to other teams during competion to find out more about their robot. They work closely with drive team

Business: Business team is in charge of keeping track of the teams funds. They look for sponserships from other companies while making sure the team is not over spending

Drive: Drive team is in charge of operating thr robot during the game. It consist of a Drive team captin, two drivers, and a human player
