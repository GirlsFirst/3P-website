What is FTC and FRC?

A robotics competition that challenges high school student to build the best robot they can. The robots usually compete in an arena. This is not a robot battle, the goal is to score as many points.

What are the differences between FTC and FRC?

FTC builds smaller robots and is less expensive. Usually the missions of FTC and FRC are similar just adapted to the size of the both. Balls and cubes are always present so you can score points.
