About This Website

This website was created as a project for Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program by Paula Fuentes, Lizbeth Gonsalez, and Julia Rodano. All three girls were participants in either FTC or FRC. Because they have always had dificulties understanding what participating in FTC/FRC encompassed, they created this website to help new members.

About Paula Fuentes

Paula Fuentes attends Donald M. Payne School of Technology as a high school junior. She is currently taking Graphic Design courses and being part of Model U.N. and Robotics Club. Her FIRST robotics team's number is 5573: Payne Bots. She and her team participated in FTC only. Paula hopes to attend Princeton University to study environmental engineering.
