About This Website

This website was created as a project for the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program.

by Paula Fuentes, Lizbeth Gonzalez, and Julia Rodano. All three girls were.

participants in either FTC or FRC. They have always had dificulties

understanding what participating in FTC/FRC encompassed, they created this website to help new members.
